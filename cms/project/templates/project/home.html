Capstone Management System


Focus on the process of Capstone Project Application and Defense, which includes the following:

  • Identifying Eligible Students, Possible Advisers and Panelists
  • Selecting Groupmates, Advisers, and Panelists
  • Selecting and Tracking of Topics
  • Submitting of Capstone Project Defense Application
  • Scheduling of Defense (Topic, Design, Preliminary, Final)
  • Tracking of Defense Status
  • Report Generation providing meaningful insights
    • # total advisers
    • # total students enrolled
    • # total groups
    • # topics of each group
    • Defense success/fail record/ current progress
